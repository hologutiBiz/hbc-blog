NELFund Onboarded 36 State-Owned Tertiary Institutions

In a significant move to broaden educational opportunities, the Nigerian Education Loan Fund (NELFund) has declared that students from 36 state-owned tertiary institutions are now eligible to apply for student loans. This announcement, effective as of July 7, 2024, marks a pivotal moment for higher education in Nigeria.

The NELFund's initiative aims to alleviate the financial burden on students pursuing higher education. By integrating these institutions into the NELFund Student Verification System (SVS), the management has streamlined the process, ensuring a more efficient and accessible loan application experience for students.

The inclusion of these institutions signifies NELFund's commitment to supporting students' aspirations and educational goals. The fund encourages other state-owned tertiary institutions to follow suit and submit their student data to the SVS promptly, enabling more students to benefit from this vital financial support.

This development is not just a win for the students but also for the educational landscape of Nigeria. It represents a step towards creating a more equitable environment where financial constraints are less of a barrier to accessing quality education.

Students from the listed institutions are encouraged to take advantage of this opportunity to finance their education and invest in their futures. With the support of NELFund, the path to academic success is now more accessible than ever.
